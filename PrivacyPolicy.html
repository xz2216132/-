Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

Welcome to Cocoon Break (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. Cocoon Break operates entirely anonymously. You can use the App without creating an account or logging in.

a. Subscription Data: Access to the App requires an active subscription. The transaction is securely handled by Apple and our revenue management provider (RevenueCat). We do not collect, process, or store your credit card information. We only receive an anonymous identifier associated with your Apple device to grant you access to the App's content and services.

b. Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated diagnostic data (such as crash logs and general device information) to improve the stability of our App.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any anonymous data we collect is strictly used to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality.
  • Verify your active subscription status to grant access to the App.
  • Analyze App performance and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

The App utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to verify your subscription entitlements. These include:

  • Apple (for processing in-app purchases)
  • RevenueCat (for managing anonymous subscription entitlements)
We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
